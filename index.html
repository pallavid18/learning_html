Independence Day, celebrated on August 15th, marks India's freedom from British rule in 1947. This day commemorates the sacrifices of freedom fighters and the end of a long struggle for independence. The Prime Minister hoists the national flag at the Red Fort in Delhi, followed by a speech and cultural programs. It's a day of national pride, unity, and remembrance of those who fought for India's freedom, urging citizens to uphold the values of the nation.
